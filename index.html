Timeline

  • 21 April: Finale Night

Application period: 5 to 12 March

Your team should write your preliminary submission on this template,
and upload it at this link by 12 March.

You will need to submit your answers to the following questions:

  1. Company name
  2. Team members (First Name, Last Name, email address, affiliation with SUTD)
  3. Summarize the market and potential creation and capture of value. This should be your elevator pitch. (500 characters with spaces)
  4. Describe the problem and market you are addressing. (1000 characters with spaces)
  5. Describe the value creation proposition. (1000 characters with spaces)
  6. Define the value capture proposition. (1000 characters with spaces)
  7. Who are your potential competitors? (1000 characters with spaces)
  8. Explain how each team member will distinctively help the business venture. (1000 characters with spaces)
  9. What actions or steps will you take to engage with customers and optimize the product-market fit in the next 3-6 months? (1000 characters with spaces)
  10. Optional additional 3 A4 pages of material (PDF upload) to be considered by judges.


Each application will be reviewed by 3-5 outside judges, who will rank applications on three criteria:

  • Value Creation: Has the team clearly identified a significant problem, and compellingly articulated a scalable solution to it?
  • Value Capture: Has the team persuasively articulated how it will earn revenue from the market's adoption of its solution?
  • Credibility: Will the team be able to leverage the prize money to succeed in their plan?

Main Competition: 24 March to 21 April

8-12 teams will be selected as finalists. Your team will be paired will mentors from startups and industry, and you'll spend three weeks working with your mentor on your own time to refine your business model and develop your pitch. At the end of this stage, each team must have a 5-page business plan and a four minute pitch.

Final Pitch

You'll pitch your business idea to a panel of experienced judges, including venture capitalists, startup founders, and industry experts.

Your pitch and 5-page business plan will be judged on four metrics:

  • Value Creation: Has the team clearly identified a significant problem, and compellingly articulated a scalable solution to it?
  • Value Capture: Has the team persuasively articulated how it will earn revenue from the market's adoption of its solution?
  • Credibility: Will the team be able to leverage the prize money to succeed in their plan?
  • Quality of Plan: Has this team determined an appropriate, financially viable plan to successfully commercialize this product?

The team with the highest score will win SGD$10,000, no strings attached.
That means your IP remains yours.

Team Rules

  1. There is no fee for entry.
  2. Individuals or teams may submit ideas.
  3. Each team may enter one idea.
  4. Each team must contain at least one SUTD affiliate. An SUTD affiliate is defined as a current SUTD student, an SUTD alumni, or an SUTD postdoc or researcher.
  5. SUTD students must submit their @mymail.sutd.edu.sg or @sutd.edu.sg email address in their submission.
  6. Required events must be attended by at least one SUTD affiliate from each team.
  7. Entries must be the original work of entrants.
  8. Teams must disclose any funding received at the time of submission.
  9. Teams must not have accepted any institutional funding for the idea being presented (i.e. the team cannot have sold equity to anyone outside of the team, including non-managing founders)
  10. Teams must not have accepted any non-institutional funding in excess of S$100,000. Non-institutional funding includes contests, grants, friends and family, bank loans, etc. where there is no equity ownership associated with the funding.
  11. Some forms of funding that fall into a gray area. In this case the SUTD 10K organizing committee will deliberate on eligibility and communicate a decision to the team. This decision on eligibility is final and cannot be appealed.
  12. The Judging Panel reserves the right to disqualify any entry based on the prior guidelines.
